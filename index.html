FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a jet ski?

$500 per day per jet ski.

Is there a deposit?

Yes, a $250 deposit is required per jet ski.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the reservation time, or the deposit is non-refundable.

Is fuel included?

No, fuel is the customer’s responsibility.

Are life jackets included?

Yes, life jackets are included.

Is there a minimum age to rent?

Yes, renters must be 21 or older.

What do I need to rent?

A valid ID, signed waiver, and payment/deposit are required before release.

What happens if the jet ski is damaged?

Renters are financially responsible for damage or loss that occurs during the rental period, as outlined in the rental agreement.

Do you deliver?

Yes, delivery is available depending on location. Delivery fees vary by location.

Can I call or text to reserve?

Yes. Call or text us directly at (417) 592-0300.
