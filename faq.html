FAQ

How do I get a quote?

Choose “Request a Quote” on the contact form and share your location, goals, and timeline.

Can you support pre-construction decisions?

Yes. We can support planning, prioritization, and scope clarity before build starts.

Can I combine renovation consulting with solar planning?

Absolutely. Multi-service projects are a core offering.

Do you offer solar maintenance subscriptions?

Yes. We can provide ongoing solar maintenance plans that help homeowners and property teams stay ahead of performance issues and routine care.

How often should solar panels be cleaned?

Cleaning frequency depends on site conditions, but many systems benefit from inspection-driven cleaning at least once or twice a year.

What are the benefits of ongoing solar care?

Ongoing solar care supports system efficiency, helps catch small issues early, and protects long-term energy savings and equipment life.

How do you handle solar troubleshooting?

We start with a structured review of system symptoms, performance data, and site conditions, then recommend the right repair, service, or next step.

How does 3D modeling help with custom builds?

3D modeling helps custom-build clients test layouts, visualize design choices, and resolve coordination questions before construction moves forward.

Is 3D modeling useful for spec homes?

Yes. For spec homes, modeling helps standardize decisions, improve presentation quality, and reduce uncertainty for builders and buyers.

How early should I start 3D modeling for new construction?

The earlier, the better. Starting during early planning gives more flexibility to refine layouts, materials, and system coordination before costs increase.

How does consulting help align construction timelines?

Consulting helps align scope, sequencing, and decision deadlines so contractors, owners, and vendors can stay coordinated around a realistic project schedule.
